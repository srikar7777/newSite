About Us

The UCF University Association is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering connections between students, alumni, and the community through sports and educational initiatives.

Founded in 1985, we've been serving the university community for over 35 years, organizing events, providing scholarships, and creating opportunities for personal and professional growth.
